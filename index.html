DJing!

In my spare time I enjoy producing and DJing electronic music
I think it is great fun.

Some of my favourite places to watch/listen to great Dj sets are...

  • Keep Hush
  • Rinse FM
  • DJ Mag

Keep Hush is a similar concept to 'Boiler Room' but it platforms a broader specrtum of underground music, which makes it more appealing to me.
Rinse FM is a legendary London based radio station that is one of the beacons of undeground UK music.
Lastly, DJ Mag, is a monthly magazine all about the world of electronic music and DJ's.

Some of my personal favourite DJ's/Artists include:

  2. Tim Reaper
  3. Mia Koden
